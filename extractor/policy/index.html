Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ZIP RAR Extractor we collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Files and Archives: The app accesses archive files (ZIP, RAR, 7Z, TAR, etc.) that you choose to extract or compress. These files are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device, including device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the app, such as features accessed and app interactions.
  • Storage Access: The app requires access to your device storage to read, extract, and create archive files.

1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To provide file extraction, compression, and archive management services
  • Storage Access: To read, write, and manage archive files on your device
  • App Improvement: To analyze app usage and improve features and performance
  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Technical Support: To diagnose and fix technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Processing: All file extraction and compression operations are performed locally on your device
  • No Cloud Upload: Your files and archives are NOT uploaded to our servers or any cloud service
  • Security: We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access

4. Permissions We Request

The app requires the following permissions:

Storage Permissions

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA_* - To access and read archive files on your device
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE - To extract files and create compressed archives

Network Permissions

  • INTERNET - To display advertisements and check for app updates
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE - To optimize ad delivery based on network availability

These permissions are essential for the app's core functionality. We do not access or collect files beyond what is necessary for providing our services.
